About me

Let me introduce myself

Hi I am Alexandre Mouriec

I am working at Argos VR Systems, a startup working on a 3D capture system, a rig system mixing DSLR cameras and Pi raspberry cams.

I am an ex college student at ENIB, an engineering school in the city of Brest in France. There, I was studying Computer Science, Electronics and Mecatronics. I am switching schools to begin in a new one in September.

In my spare time, I am a tech writer for the website Stuffi. I write about the Internet of Things. Quantified Self, wearables, drones and VR are some of the topics I write about.
